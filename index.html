Privacy Policy for Flip the Shell

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Flip the Shell is a Chrome extension designed to detect and extract hidden data from images using steganography techniques. We take your privacy seriously.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Personal Information: Flip the Shell does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, such as your name, email address, browsing history (outside of the detection feature), or any other identifying data.

Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics services to track your usage of the extension.

2. How the Extension Works

Local Processing: All image analysis, scanning, and data extraction are performed locally on your device within your browser. No image data or extracted content is ever sent to our servers or any third parties.

Permissions: The extension requires specific permissions (activeTab, scripting, storage, all_urls) to function. These are used solely to scan images on the pages you visit for "SNAIL" signatures and to bypass CORS restrictions for technical analysis.

3. Third-Party Links

Our extension and documentation may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., GitHub, Chrome Web Store). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through our GitHub repository.
